Create a room and share the link with your family members

How to Use This App

  1. Click "Create Room" to start a new video call
  2. Share the room link with your family members
  3. Ask them to click the link to join your call
  4. You may need to allow camera and microphone access
  5. Click "End Call" when you're finished

Note: This is a simplified demonstration. For a production app, you would need to implement signaling and STUN/TURN servers.
